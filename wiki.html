How do I make money?

There are many ways to earn money on RageMines. Some of the most common methods include:

  • Link your Discord account using /discordlink and use the server tag to receive hourly rewards.
  • Claim your Daily Milestones using /dms.
  • Trade Beacons obtained throughout your progression.
  • Vote for the server to receive rewards.
  • Use /order to sell valuable items to other players.
  • Sell items on the Auction House using /ah.
  • Participate in events like Boat Races, Spleef, and King of the Hill (KOTH).
  • Join Chat Games for free rewards.
  • Trade directly with other players.
  • Purchase and open Crates, located on the left side of Spawn.
!
Tip

Selling valuable progression materials through /order or the Auction House is often one of the fastest ways to earn money.

New player guide

Getting Started

This guide covers the RageMines basics: where to go first, how progression works, and what to do after finishing Overworld.

01
Your first tools

First Steps

When you first join RageMines, the first mine is directly in front of you. Start by using your axe to gather wood.

  1. 1Gather wood from the first mine.
  2. 2Use the wood to buy your first armor set, sword, and pickaxe.
  3. 3Keep upgrading your pickaxe—it becomes your main progression tool for most of the server.
!
Beginner tip

Prioritize your pickaxe after buying your starter equipment. You will rely on it throughout your journey.

02
Quick access

Useful Early Commands

These commands cover food, useful items, PvP settings, and asking other players for help.

/miscUtility

Use /misc if you run out of food. This area is also where you can get an Elytra and find the Talisman.

T
Using the Talisman

The Talisman gives a strong buff to speed, damage, and health. It must be placed in your offhand to work.

Java Use your assigned offhand key Bedrock Use /offhand
/pvpRisk & reward

Use /pvp to turn PvP on or off. With PvP enabled, you receive 2× Fortune on everything you mine, making progression faster.

Know the risk PvP enabled means other players can attack you. In general, keeping it enabled is best for faster progression.
/orderPlayer help

Use /order to ask for help or place an order for an item you need. If you are confused, you can also ask the community directly in chat.

Do not get stuck RageMines players can often point you toward the right mine, NPC, or next upgrade.
03
Core progression

Understanding Mines

P
Pickaxe Mines

Most mines use your pickaxe. It is your main progression tool, so expect to upgrade and rely on it throughout the server.

T
Tool Mines

Around one or two mines use another tool. These mines are generally harder than the pickaxe mines.

04
After the last mine

Finishing Overworld

After reaching the last Overworld mine, your next goal is the Overworld boss.

  • The boss area is in the middle of Overworld, behind the wood mine.
  • Look for the Overworld Boss portal shown here.
  • Talk to the Golem NPC next to the portal to view the Overworld trade.
  • The trade gives the final version of the Overworld gear.
05
Prepare the trade

Overworld Trade Requirements

The Overworld trade requires boss drops and a Cohesive Silvergrass. In Overworld, the boss drop is a Poppy.

1Return to the start

Go back to where your Overworld progression began.

2Repeat every mine

Travel through the mines again and upgrade your Cohesive at each one.

3Reach Silvergrass

Continue until your Cohesive has been upgraded to Cohesive Silvergrass.

i
Where is the Cohesive trade?

Find the Baby Villager NPC all the way at the bottom.

Video walkthrough

Cohesive Silvergrass Tutorial

Community standards

Server Rules

Help keep RageMines fun and fair for everyone by following these rules.

Staff reserve the right to interpret and enforce rules based on context, intent, and the overall impact on the server. Punishments are based on previous offenses and severity.

Quick navigation
Cheating & Fair Play Chat Rules Trading & Scamming PvP & Gameplay Accounts Helpful Notes
Fair playCheating & Fair Play

  • No hacking. You cannot use a hacked client.

  • Auto-fishing is considered hacking.

  • Auto-clicking is bannable outside of Spawn or the AFK Area.

  • Using duplicated items is bannable.

  • No bug abusing. Reporting bugs rewards you with a Bug Token.

  • No AFK mining (except inside the AFK World).

  • No external trading.

CommunicationChat Rules

  • No spamming or flooding chat. Allowed in Team Chat.

  • English only in public chat. Non-English is allowed in Team Chat.

  • No NSFW content. Not allowed in Team Chat either.

  • No racism, slurs, religious discrimination, or death threats.

  • Common slurs, including the N-word, F-slur, and R-slur, are prohibited. Not allowed in Team Chat.

  • No excessive or offensive swearing directed at another player. General swearing is allowed, but targeted harassment is not.

  • No toxicity.

  • No political or religious discussions in public chat.

Player tradesTrading & Scamming

  • No advertising. Not allowed in Team Chat. Private messages (PMs) are allowed.

  • Scamming is only allowed if the Server Store is not involved and no Pickaxe or Epic+ Reforge Stone is involved.

  • If a Pickaxe or Epic+ Reforge Stone is involved in a scam, only that Pickaxe or Stone will be returned. The remainder of the trade will not be reversed.

  • Staff will not help move items between players.

CombatPvP & Gameplay

  • No kill farming using alternate accounts, friends, or any other arrangement.

  • Naked killing is allowed.

  • Kill spamming is only punished by the server console.

01
Your first world

Overview

Every journey, system, and first upgrade begins here.

The Overworld is the very first world every player starts in. Everything begins here, including progression, gathering resources, unlocking gear, and learning the mechanics of RageMines.

It also acts as the main hub for all worlds before Prestige 2, giving players one familiar place to return to while they advance.

Begin here, return often

Learn the fundamentals, meet key NPCs, and use the portals around the world to continue your adventure.

i
Exploration is always open

Players are free to explore every unlocked portal even if they do not yet meet that world's Prestige requirement. NPC trades inside higher Prestige worlds remain locked until the required Prestige is reached.

02
A good first adventure

Tips & Tricks

There is no need to rush through your first world.

01Explore the world.
02Talk to NPCs.
03Don't be afraid to ask questions in chat.
04Use /order if you need help obtaining items.
05Learn the server mechanics naturally.
06Visit every area around Spawn.
07Experiment and have fun.

The Overworld is designed to teach the foundations of RageMines. Take your time, explore, meet other players, and enjoy discovering everything the server has to offer.

01 World progressionOverworld 1 enemy
Overworld Boss

Reinforced Golem

BossEasy

The Reinforced Golem is the player's first boss encounter. It has moderate health and moderate damage, making it an introduction to boss mechanics.

AttackBasic melee attacks

Stay aware of your health while trading hits.

SummonSmall Golem minions

Clear minions before they crowd the fight.

ControlPlayer pull

The boss can pull nearby players toward itself.

Strategy

Fight it in melee while keeping an eye on your health. If you need to heal, hide behind terrain to avoid being pulled toward the boss.

  • This is a forgiving boss intended to teach the basics.
  • Use the terrain as a recovery tool whenever you need space.
02 World progressionCave 1 enemy
Cave Boss

Mega Warden

BossHard Early Game

The Mega Warden is extremely tanky and deals high damage, making it a major early-game wall for unprepared players.

AttackPowerful melee

Getting too close without healing ready is dangerous.

Range punishWarden Beam

Used when players are far away.

Mobility checkUnderground burrow

The burrow launches players into the air and can be avoided with a well-timed Grappling Hook.

Strategy

For early-game players, the safest strategy is to fight with a bow. Run around the arena in circles while continuously shooting the boss.

  • Keep moving to reduce the chance of being hit by its abilities.
  • Time the Grappling Hook against the burrow attack instead of reacting late.

Ninja

MobEasy

Ninjas are fast melee enemies that attack before quickly dashing away. Eight Ninjas can spawn at the same time.

PatternFast melee

They close distance quickly.

MovementDash away

They disengage after attacking.

Sakura Mini Boss

Samurai Panda

Mini BossMedium

The Samurai Panda is very slow but extremely powerful.

AttackHeavy melee

Do not stay in range after committing to a hit.

TellSlow attack animations

The long windup creates a clear dodge window.

Strategy

Defeat it safely with a bow, or bait its attacks before moving in with your sword.

Sakura Boss

The Lovers

BossMedium

The Lovers is the first three-phase boss players encounter.

Phase 1
Hanashiguro

A slow melee fighter whose most dangerous attack throws multiple Kunai before jumping backwards. Use the Grappling Hook to dodge.

Phase 2
The Lovers

A Nymph appears. At close range she uses a Trident; at long range she uses Charged Arrow and Arrow Rain.

Phase 3
Final Form

Hanashiguro breaks his armor and becomes extremely fast. The Nymph throws Tridents capable of stunning players.

RangeKunai and bow abilities

Stay mobile and use the Grappling Hook when needed.

ControlStunning Tridents

Avoid incoming Tridents during the final phase.

Strategy

Fight Phase 2 from range using a bow while continuously moving around the arena. Phase 3 strongly favors aggressive melee, with retreats when low on health.

Recommended progression

Beginner Recommendations

  1. 1
    Buy a Pickaxe Fortune Stone

    Make this your first Reforge purchase.

  2. 2
    Upgrade it whenever possible

    Keep improving it throughout your progression.

  3. 3
    Buy a Tool Fortune Stone

    Prepare for tool-based mines and later progression.

  4. 4
    Upgrade both Fortune stones

    Maintain both stones as you advance.

  5. 5
    Invest in HP stones later

    Add survivability after your Fortune setup is established.

  6. 6
    Reach the Magic World

    Obtain the Cooldown Reduction Stone once you meet its progression requirement.
